Triumph Crest Key



This striking key is a must for all Triumph owners. The TR marque is stamped in relief to form the head of this vintage style key. Keys are highly polished solid brass. They are sent as blanks, and can be cut at any local key shop for a nominal charge. Be sure to check fit before cutting. Available in two lock styles; FS and FP.

GT6 '64 through '68.....ignition, door, trunk
