Radiator Shroud

For 1962-1976 MGB's



Image of shroud on MGA

Make your radiator more efficient and help prevent overheating in hot, city traffic with this shroud! These shrouds are on virtually all modern vehicles, and for good reason.

Shrouds create a sealed chamber between your fan and radiator, thereby pulling vacuum across the entire radiator, not just the center where the fan is.

Good looking, quality part the factory should have provided. Black, high-impact polyethylene. Almost invisible when installed. Mounts using existing radiator bolts.
